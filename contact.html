Contacting Owen

I very much welcome contact with fans. I’ll gladly answer questions about my novels or chat about the writing process. Please also feel free to politely report typos in my work.

Business inquiries may also be E-Mailed, but unless they’re directly related to my work as a writer, they will be deleted without response. For example, if you want to do a TV or movie adaptation of my work, feel free to reach out. On the other hand, if you’re just trying to sell me something, your message will be filed as spam.

I’m NOT interested in hearing from advertisers looking to promote my work for a fee. I’ve got own plans for promotion and if I receive such a message from you, I’m likely to pay special attention to it and note the name of your company, to ensure I never do business with you, because you clearly ignored these instructions.

In short, DO NOT send me spam of any kind.

You can reach me via E-Mail at owentyme@owentyme.us.

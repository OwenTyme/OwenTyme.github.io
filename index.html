About Owen

2023-10-19 2:57 pm

Though he currently calls Liberal, Kansas home, Owen Tyme was born in the California Bay Area. He's come to enjoy the mild climate of Kansas.  He's a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints.

Owen prefers to write action-filled science fantasy, though he sometimes writes fantasy or science fiction, when the inspiration takes him there. He loves grounding what he writes in science, even when writing about dragons, witches and wizards.

His favorite authors include Terry Pratchett, R. A. Salvatore, Douglas Adams and Isaac Asimov.  More recently, he's discovered a liking for Mercedes Lackey, as well.  He loves novels grounded in scientific truth, but also loves a little flight of fancy.  He especially loves it when the two work hand-in-hand, as in the works of Pratchett.

Books

2023-10-19 2:58 pm

Any of the below books that shows a cover image is available for purchase, though it may only be available as a pre-order, in which case the tentative release date will be listed.  Clicking the images will take you to the books2read.com page for the book, where you can read more and/or follow a link to a store page.


The Wizard's Scion (Series) #

The Wizard's Scion is a series centered around a young wizard (Levi Jacobs), who's the son of the greatest wizard his world has ever seen.  It follows the adventures of this young man, his family and his friends, as he slowly learns to master his wizardly powers over the course of the series.

The entirety of this series has already been written and each awaits only the completion of cover art, in order to be released.


Troll Song

Lyra, a teenage troll, makes telepathic contact with a dwarf and learns that life doesn't have to be full of abuse.  Turning on her evil family, she seeks to protect the world from the troll menace, only to gain the ire of her older sister, Nicole, an incredibly tenacious and deadly swordswoman.  Lyra freezes the trolls in ice and a teen wizard, Levi Jacobs, inadvertently releases Nicole, thousands of years later.  Lyra teams up with Levi, his family and friends to end the troll threat, once and for all.

Troll Song is currently available as an eBook from a number of retailers.  Learn more here.


Forgotten Legends

Ancient, forgotten gods and monsters begin a war with humanity, with Levi's family unknowingly caught in the middle.


The Third Wish

Old enemies of Levi's family obtain the power to travel through time and seek to alter history to suit themselves, threatening to upset the history of the entire galaxy.


Dark Moon

When the usually invisible moon of Levi's home world makes an appearance in the sky and ancient 'Harbingers of Doom' begin harvesting life from the world, Levi and a hated nemesis are forced to work together, lest they both lose everything they love.


The Inverted Glass

In the concluding volume of The Wizard's Scion, Levi faces tragedy beyond anything he's ever faced before, losing both his wife and firstborn child shortly after her birth.  Reeling from the loss, he staggers though life, losing nearly everything he cares about.  In his darkest moment, he's reminded that he's destined to become a time traveler.  Can he restore everything he's lost by fighting fate itself?


Jigsaw City (Series)
#

Jigsaw City is a sequel series to The Wizard's Scion, centering around the life of Levi's daughter, Nicole Jacobs, and her quest to reunite the pieces of a magical city that was broken up millions of years before she was born.

The first novel of this series has been written and is simply waiting for The Wizard's Scion series to be published.  The other two have yet to be written.


Jigsaw City

Nicole Jacobs was given a small, hexagonal coin for her twentieth birthday, but during the analysis of the strange, magic trinket, she accidentally copies the magic item's base spell into her mind, where it rapidly expands beyond its original design, slowly consuming her mind from the inside!  Nicole reluctantly seeks out the maker of the item, hoping to find a cure before it's too late to save her sanity.


Collector of Kurg

Having accepted the fact that the City of Kurg is permanently bonded to her mind, Nicole seeks to collect all of the missing fragments of the magical city, both on and off her home planet.


The Great Purpose

In the third volume of Jigsaw City, the true purpose of the magical city is revealed: it was designed as a powerful weapon, for fighting a war that's as old as the universe.


Lost City

Having gone on a suicide mission to save the galaxy from an existential threat, Nicole sacrifices her life to save everyone she loves.  Her body is restored by automatic functions of the City of Kurg, but her mind is shattered by the experience.  Lost, confused and alone, will Nicole ever return to her home and those she loves or will she forever wander a dying, parallel universe, seeking a home she can't even remember?  


Puzzle Pieces #

Puzzle Pieces is a series in name only.  Each novel introduces concepts and characters that will be used in the Jigsaw City series, though they are otherwise independent.

Fey Fighters  and She Who Hunts Demons have already been written and are simply waiting for the appropriate time to be published.


Fey Fighters

On a distant, unexplored planet, war breaks out between humans that are forced to land there and the fairies that left Earth during World War 2, because the human race was becoming too violent for their tastes.

Fey Fighters introduces true fairies to the setting, who will also be seen in Collector of Kurg (Volume 2 of Jigsaw City), where they will play a vital role providing exposition and will likely also be a part of The Great Purpose (Volume 3 of Jigsaw City), due to the unusual nature of both demons and fey.


She Who Hunts Demons

On the backdrop of New York City in 1945, a half-demon girl with an ax to grind with demon-kind over the way they murdered her parents seeks to stop them from harming humanity.  Aided by a group of human demon hunters, they seek to thwart the millennia-spanning plans of a demon that claims to be the serpent from the Garden of Eden.

The Hunter will also be seen in The Great Purpose (Volume 3 of Jigsaw City).


The Hunter Goes to War

With the return of her arch-nemesis to the world, in 1972, the Hunter literally goes to war, finding herself drawn to Vietnam, once more backed up by her demon hunting allies.

This novel will draw on the real experience of a Vietnam war veteran, who happens to be the author's friend.


The Japanese Hunt (Potential Novel)

When a powerful demon-witch appears in present-day Japan, the Hunter begins a search-and-destroy mission that crosses through famous cities of the Land of the Rising Sun.

The Japanese Hunt is a book that Owen hasn't yet made up his mind about.  It's only a potential novel, because Owen has no direct experience with a visit to Japan.  To get the descriptive details right, he would either have to spend a great deal of time interviewing someone that has or would have to take a research vacation there.  Unfortunately, Owen doesn't have the money for that.


Smart Bomb

Having obtained an illegal level of intelligence, comparable to that of a human, a robot seeks to hide and survive on the streets of Neo York City on the planet Neo York, deep within United Nations of the South Galaxy territory.  While on the run, it stumbles across a plot to develop a dangerous, new weapon: a hand-held anti-matter explosive.  Acting on a deep, moral imperative, the robot decides to do something about it.  However, the robot isn't the only one on the trail, stumbling into the Hunter, who's out to kill the demon-scientist developing the bomb for the purpose of opening a portal to the home of demon-kind.  Will they team up or tear each other apart?

This novel will be set about 300 years before the time of Zechariah Jacobs.


Sky Children (Series) #

Sky Children is a prequel series to The Wizard's Scion, being largely about the adventures of Levi's father, Zechariah Jacobs, an electrical engineer that crashed on a supposedly unexplored planet, which happened to give him magical powers, making him a wizard.

This series is currently incomplete, needing serious attention and editing before it will be ready for publishing.


Sky Children

Zechariah crash lands on an 'unexplored' planet, only to uncover a terrifying government conspiracy to hide the existence of the Mind Fire virus, which grants magic powers to those infected by it.


Sky War

As a result of revealing the existence of the Mind Fire virus to the public, Zechariah's new home world catches the eye of his old nation, the Northwestern Empire, which sends an invasion fleet of ships, thousands strong, to wipe out the evidence, so the truth can be erased.


Wild Sky

Having settled things with the Northwestern Empire, Zechariah is contacted by the Empire's new empress, who was on his side of the war.  With a civil war looming, due to Zechariah's actions, he and his people step up to aid her.  However, as things grow ever more dire, Zechariah seeks to achieve ever greater feats of magic and falls to the temptation of using rage magic, a dangerously addictive, but incredibly powerful form of magic.

Contacting Owen

2023-10-19 2:58 pm

E-Mail #

You can reach Owen Tyme via E-Mail at owentyme at owentyme dot us.

Owen welcomes contact with fans.  He'll gladly answer questions about his novels or chat about the writing process, in a general sense.  Please also feel free to politely report typos in his work.

Business inquiries may also be E-Mailed, but unless they're directly related to Owen's work as a writer, they will be deleted without response.  For example, if you want to do a TV or movie adaptation of his work, feel free to reach out.  On the other hand, if you're just trying to sell him something, your message will be filed as spam and then deleted, without reply.

Owen is NOT interested in hearing from advertisers looking to promote his work, for a fee.  He already has his own plans for promotion and if he receives such a message from you, he's likely to pay special attention to it and note the name of your company, to ensure he never does business with you, because you clearly ignored these instructions.

In short, DO NOT send Owen spam of any kind.

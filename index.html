About Owen

2023-04-10 2:16 am

Owen Tyme is the recipient of several honorable mentions from Writers of the Future, including having once reached the quarter finals.


Though he currently resides in Kansas, he was born in the California Bay Area, spent many years in Utah and once upon a time, for a short period, lived in Georgia.  He's come to enjoy the mild climate of Kansas and is happy to have found a corner of the state where tornados are rare.


Owen has had a life-long love of both fantasy and science fiction.  His favorite authors include Terry Pratchett, R. A. Salvatore, Douglas Adams and Isaac Asimov.  More recently, he's discovered a liking for Mercedes Lackey, as well.  He loves novels grounded in scientific truth, but also loves a little flight of fancy.  He especially loves it when the two work hand-in-hand, as in the works of Pratchett.

Sky Children #

Science Fiction and Fantasy Author

I’m Owen Tyme, a novelist that writes fantasy and science fiction. I’m especially fond of mixing the two genres. I write both novels and short stories.

To learn more, click one of the below covers for my published works or follow one of the links on this page.

Ashen Blades

Ashen Blades centers around the life of a half-demon girl that calls herself the Hunter, whose parents were murdered by demons. Joining her crusade against demon-kind are the Ashen Blades, also known as the Order of Ash and Smoke, an organization of demon hunters.

Sky Children (Coming Soon)

Sky Children is a prequel series to The Wizard’s Scion, centered on the adventures of Zechariah Jacobs, an electrical engineer that crashes on a supposedly unexplored planet, which happens to give him magical powers via a mysterious virus, making him a wizard.

The Wizard’s Scion

The Wizard’s Scion follows the life of a great and powerful wizard’s son, Levi Jacobs, as he grows from a teenage boy with almost no control over his magic to a wizard of great power and reputation, that masters the secrets of time travel.

Short Stories (Free Ebooks!)

Short of Tyme is a series of short stories that you can find alongside my novels, in the same online stores. If you’re on the fence about buying my books, then why not try something free that’s a little shorter?

In particular, She Goes to Summer Camp will give you a glimpse into the Ashen Blades series, since it’s part of it.
